The open door

The Open Door is a new venture by the team behind the Way Inn. At 360-364 High Street, HP4 1HU, just five minutes walk from the Upstairs Gallery, it is a creative centre for the community to use. The large downstairs area is gallery/project space for artists, community groups and charities. A programme of creative workshops is running in the large upstairs room. In addition there are a number of smaller rooms suitable for a variety of uses, plus kitchen facilities. Events and activities include:

  • Gallery space
  • Group activities
  • Classes: such as Tai Chi, art, bridge, music, computing....
  • Coffee mornings
  • Charity fundraising events
  • Internet (cafe) for home-based workers
  • Children's activities
  • Drop-in sessions where you can meet new people
  • Mother & baby groups
  • Meeting rooms for hire

We are open to other suggestions; please let us know on the email address below.
