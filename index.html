About

The Upstairs Gallery was set up in 2011, supported by a charitable trust and aims to provide a showcase for Chiltern artists at an affordable cost. The gallery is situated in a light, welcoming space above the Post Office in central Berkhamsted. Throughout the year the gallery has themed open submission exhibitions, solo / partner shows, and exhibits work by local community/education groups.

The Gallery is a volunteer-run organisation for the benefit of the regional art community. If you have an interest in art, like meeting people or just enjoy the atmosphere within the gallery environment, we would love to hear from you. If you'd like to join our team, please email us at upstairsgalleryberkhamsted@gmail.com
